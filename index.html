NUTRITION & DIET

Fuel your body for optimal performance and recovery.

Hydration is Key

Drink at least 3-4 liters of water daily, especially around workout times.

Protein Intake

Aim for 1.6-2.2g of protein per kg of body weight to build and repair muscle.

Carb Timing

Consume complex carbs 2 hours before training and simple carbs post-workout.

Sample Muscle-Building Diet

  • Breakfast: Oatmeal with whey protein, berries, and peanut butter.
  • Lunch: Grilled chicken breast, sweet potato, and broccoli.
  • Pre-Workout: Banana and black coffee.
  • Post-Workout: Protein shake and a rice cake.
  • Dinner: Salmon, quinoa, and asparagus.
